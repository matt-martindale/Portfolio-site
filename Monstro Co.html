Monstro Co.

Started in 2016, Monstro Co. is a way to create lifestyle products that can be personalized with Morse code.

First I began with a poster design that people could choose 40 characters to send a message in Morse code. Then I designed a twin-layer Men's bracelet called the "Dualis". People could choose 2 letters or numbers with which to customize their bracelets.

With our Men's dual-layer bracelet you can customize it with 2 letters or numbers to symbolize anything you think, whatever meaning they have to you; a loved ones initials, special saying, your secret superhero name, whatever!
